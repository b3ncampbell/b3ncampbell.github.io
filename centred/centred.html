Centred Layout

This is a simple centred layout that I've made. The purpose of this page is so that I can practise responsive design.

On a phone you should see four boxes stacked in the blue area, one on top of the other.

On a tablet you should see the boxes stacked two on top of the other two.

On a large screen you should see the boxes all on one row.

If you're on a desktop, resize your browser window to see the boxes stack as your window gets smaller.

The links below also change how they are displayed depending on the screen size. Vertically on a phone, two lists above one on a tablet, and horizontally on a large screen.
